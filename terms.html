Objective Laundry Solutions strives to give excellent customer service first time, every time. Due to the human nature of the operations, there might be instances where the perfect service we strive for is not met. This policy aims to address this gap and define recourse a customer may have in the case of where less than perfect service is provided.
Policy As we work to towards providing service excellence, Laundry Solutions wants to give customers the assurance and peace of mind that any laundry brought into our laundromat will come back in better condition than when it was brought in. In the instance that it is not, the customer is entitled to the below. Where there is clear negligence from the part of Laundry Solutions and there is damage to the customers clothing, Laundry Solutions shall pay the customer the below.
• Where the item/items have not been used before (new) 100% cost of replace the item. Provided that the customer can provide proof of repurchase within 30 days of the incident.
• Where the item has been worn before 50% cost to replace the item. Provided that the customer can provide proof of repurchase within 30 days of the incident.
The maximum repayment for any claim is capped at R500.
What determines a valid claim?
• Claim is made within 5 days of the service provided.
• Proof that the service was provided to the customer (Invoice).
• Laundry Solutions can confirm that the item was indeed processed at the date provided in the claim.
• Clear negligence on the part of Laundry Solutions can determined.
